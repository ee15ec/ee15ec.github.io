About me

I graduated from the University of Leeds in 2018 with a BSc(Hons) in Environmental Science. I am currently studying for an MRes in Climate and Atmospheric Science at the University of Leeds. My research project this year is focussed on quantifying the distribution of rainfall from monsoon depressions and low pressure systems for the Indian Monsoon.

I have created this website for my Programming for "Geographical Information Analysis: Core Skills" module. Here I will create links to my coding practicals and the final agent based model I create using techniques learnt throughout the course.
